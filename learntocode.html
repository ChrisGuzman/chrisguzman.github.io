Learn to code in Baltimore/DC for free or cheap.

by Chris Guzman

Everyone talks about coding, but where should you go to learn some skills? Here I'll point out upcoming classes and workshops that are free or low cost. Some of these come from Meetup.com or from other sites I see. If you see any classes you'd like to promote here contact me @speaktochris.

Rails Girls DC

Location: TBA and Silver Spring Civic Building

June 4 and 5, 2014

This summer, Rails Girls DC is hosting a free 2-day workshop leading up to RubyNation. Come to learn or teach about building web applications with Ruby on Rails. Apply here! For more info, visit their website.

Android Development for beginners

Location: Howard County Library

May 17, 2014 3:00p to 6:00p

10 members are currently taking an online course in Android Development. They meet in the Library every Saturday, to study together. Anyone is welcome, whether taking the Coursera course, studying on their own, or an expert willing to offer additional help. For more information, check out the Meetup group.

Principles of Design Patterns

Location: TEKsystems

971 Corporate Blvd #401, Linthicum, MD

Tuesday, May 20, 2014 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Group members serve as resources to each other as the explore topics related to Design Patterns. There will be a design pattern presentation examining the object-oriented principles that underline all design patterns. Then they will classify the three major categories of problems solved by design patterns. Examples will be given in both Java and C#. For more information checkout the Meetup group.

PS. I might be there!

Intro to Sass

Location: Emerging Technology Centers (ETC)

101 N. Haven Street, Baltimore, MD

Thursday, May 22, 2014 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM

Sass is the new way to wrangle your CSS. It builds upon the CSS you already know how to do while allowing you to manage your CSS in a way that is maintainable and modular. THe class will cover nesting, imports, comments and variables with Sass. Students will learn how to use these techniques and practices to create a mixin, extend to other classes, manipulate colors and math, and how to use Sass to build a simple responsive website. For more information checkout the Meetup group.

Cost: $24.00/per person
